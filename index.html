About

Dr. Navchetan Awasthi is a faculty member at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, IIT Jodhpur. His research focuses on inverse problems and computational methods for medical imaging and signal processing, with an emphasis on deep learning techniques for precision imaging in ultrasound and photoacoustic settings.

He has previously held positions at the University of Amsterdam and Eindhoven University of Technology, and was a Research Fellow at Massachusetts General Hospital under Harvard Medical School. He completed his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.
