About

Dr. Navchetan Awasthi is a faculty member at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, IIT Jodhpur. His research focuses on inverse problems and computational methods for medical imaging and signal processing, with an emphasis on deep learning techniques for precision imaging.

He has previously held positions at the University of Amsterdam and Eindhoven University of Technology, and was a Research Fellow at Massachusetts General Hospital and Harvard Medical School. He completed his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

Research Interests

Student Opportunities

I am always looking for motivated students interested in deep learning, medical imaging, and signal processing. If you are interested in joining as a Ph.D. student, postdoc, or project intern, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to my institutional email at IIT Jodhpur.

Ph.D. positions: Candidates with strong backgrounds in mathematics, computer science, or electrical engineering and interest in medical imaging are encouraged to apply.
M.Tech / B.Tech projects: IIT Jodhpur students are welcome to reach out directly.
