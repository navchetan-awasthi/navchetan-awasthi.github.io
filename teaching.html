Current Courses

School of AI & Data Science, IIT Jodhpur

Deep Learning for Medical Imaging

Postgraduate

Covers fundamentals and advanced applications of deep learning in medical image analysis. Topics include image segmentation, reconstruction, classification, and generative models for ultrasound, MRI, and photoacoustic imaging.

Computational Methods for Signal Processing

Postgraduate

Mathematical foundations of signal and image processing. Topics include inverse problems, iterative reconstruction, regularization, and optimization methods applied to biomedical signals.

Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Postgraduate

Survey of AI applications across clinical workflows including diagnosis, prognosis, treatment planning, and federated learning for healthcare data privacy.

Student Opportunities

I am always looking for motivated students interested in deep learning, medical imaging, and signal processing. If you are interested in joining as a Ph.D. student, postdoc, or project intern, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to my institutional email at IIT Jodhpur.

Ph.D. positions: Candidates with strong backgrounds in mathematics, computer science, or electrical engineering and interest in medical imaging are encouraged to apply.
M.Tech / B.Tech projects: IIT Jodhpur students are welcome to reach out directly.
